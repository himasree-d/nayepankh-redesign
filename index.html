About Us

Think global. Act local.

A wing for every cause

NayePankh Foundation began with a simple belief — that service to people is service to something larger. What started as a small student initiative has grown into one of India's biggest student-led NGOs, working directly with communities in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and several other cities.

We don't ask for grand gestures. Your money, your skills, or even an afternoon of your time — every contribution becomes part of a larger wingspan that lifts someone else.

"If we all do something, then together there is no problem that we cannot solve." — Prashant Shukla, Founder & President
What We Do

Programs built around real needs

Each initiative is shaped by what communities tell us they need most — not the other way around.

Free Food Drives

Regular meal and ration distribution for families facing food insecurity in underserved neighbourhoods.

Education Support

Free books, tutoring, and learning kits for children who would otherwise have no access to schooling.

Sanitary Pad Donation

Distributing sanitary pads and menstrual health awareness sessions for girls and women in rural areas.

Clothing Drives

Seasonal clothing collection and distribution drives to help families through winters and emergencies.
